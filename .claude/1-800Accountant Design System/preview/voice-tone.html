Voice
  • Second person — "Your books", "You'll meet"
  • Confident, warm, plainspoken — no hedging, no jargon
  • Sentence case — titles & buttons. Never ALL CAPS.
  • Oxford-free commas when terse; proper elsewhere
  • No emoji in product surfaces
Do say
"We'll handle the IRS. You get back to running the business."
Don't say
"Our proprietary AI-powered platform leverages synergies…"
